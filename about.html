About Willo

Our Mission

Willo empowers church communities to track and manage contributions across all ministry groups with transparency and ease.

What We Do

Willo is a contribution tracking system designed specifically for church ministries. We help you:

  • Track contributions for multiple ministry groups (Choir, Building Fund, Missions, etc.)
  • Monitor payment history and identify defaulted months
  • Generate annual summaries for contributors
  • Provide multiple payment options (Card, Mobile Money, Cash)
  • Send automated notifications and reminders

Our Story

Willo was created by church leaders who understood the challenges of managing contributions across multiple ministry groups. What started as a simple spreadsheet evolved into this comprehensive system to serve churches of all sizes.
